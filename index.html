About

I’m a researcher at United International University focusing on bias detection and correction in Bengali news. I care about transparent, reproducible pipelines, and building tools that help journalists and readers see how language shapes perception.

  • Focus: Bias detection, counterfactual rewriting, explainability
  • Models: BanglaBERT, mBERT, instruction-tuned LMs
  • Values: Reproducibility, clarity, human-centered evaluation
