About

An Author in the living world…

I am a person of academia, a lifelong learner, and a researcher in the ever-evolving field of Computer Science. My journey in this realm is driven by an insatiable curiosity and a profound commitment to expanding the boundaries of knowledge.

Beyond Computer Science, I nurture a deep passion for Science and Technology broadly, with particular fascination for Astrophysics, Theoretical Physics, Applied Mathematics, and History. This interdisciplinary approach enriches my perspective and informs my research methodology.
